Client

Richard E. Keene, Law Office of Minor & Keene, a P.C.

Our client runs a law office, specializing in Estate Planning. A website was built for him several years ago, but was neglected. The site is extremely simple but severely outdated with non-functional or poorly crafted components.

Project

Scope

Our project will create a new website, following a similar structure with updated and added features.

Updated features:
  • "Service" tiles to open in same page instead of generating a new page
  • Updated employee photos and Bios
  • Updated website photos
  • Updated location to include embedded, interactive map
  • Updated navigation bar to include drop-down menues
Add features:
  • Secure login
  • Document upload/download
  • Articles of Interest page

The project goal is to provide an updated website for the law office that will specifically address the issues involved with transmitting sensitive documents, while providing additional functionality for the user.
